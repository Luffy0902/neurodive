Urgent Help

If you need urgent help, there are resources available for you.

Hotlines

Follow this link for hotlines around the world, and scroll till you find your country. On the front page,the emergency number and suicide hotline number and readily available. The "More hotlines" button directs you to more of your country's hotlines in various categories, such as "youth" and "substance abuse".

Calming Activities

Visit our Sensory Calming Activities page to find activities that will calm you down when you're overwhelmed or overstimulated. It's under the resources tab!

Professional Help

If you are regularly having negative feelings, you should consider consulting with a psychologist. Book an appointment with a psychologist and see what further action suits you. This could include therapy, consulting a psychiatrist, and more based on the issues you are having. Make sure the psychologist is aware of your neurodisorder so they can advise you on a suitable course of action.

About Us

Neurodive is a website that aims to increase communication between neurodivergent people. In a world designed for neurotypical people, it is important to have a space where neurodivergent people can talk to each other and support each other. On Neurodive, you will find:

  • A community page, where you can make posts about any topic you like. People can comment under the posts as well!
  • A Sensory Calming Activities page, under the resources tab. You can find many ways to overcome sensory overload here.
  • An urgent help page, which tells you what to do in case of an emergency.

Happy Exploring!
